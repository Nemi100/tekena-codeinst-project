The Band

The Cosmic Wanderers’ journey began under a meteor shower on a moonless night. In a dimly lit basement, four musicians discovered a shared love for retro-futuristic soundscapes. Their instruments hummed like distant quasars, and the air crackled with creative energy. The lineup solidified:

  • Aurora Nova (Vocals and Synths): A celestial siren whose voice echoes across light-years.
  • Stellar Pulse (Guitar and Effects): A cosmic guitarist, fingers dancing on strings like shooting stars.
  • Nebula Groove (Bass and Beats): The pulsar heart of the band, laying down interstellar rhythms.
